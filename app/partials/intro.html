Elo Rating Sytem

The Elo rating system was originally invented by Arpad Elo to calculate skill levels of chess players. This system has been widely used as a rating system for multiplayer competition in a number of video games, football and many other games.

This app is the implementation of Elo rating system.

Each new player starts with a default rating of 1000, which will increase or decrease based upon the outcome of games.

When a match is played between players of different ratings, the lower ranked player will gain a higher rating change if he/she wins and smaller loss, when compared to the higher ranked player.
